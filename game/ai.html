FAF AI Advancements

Originally, Supreme Commander:FA had very suboptimal AI, being barely able to do anything even trivial. Nonetheless, thanks to the contribution of FAF contributors, now you have 4 quite challenging AIs! Specially on 5x5 and 10x10 land maps, these AIs will show you who's boss! You can download these AIs in the FAF client Mods section.


{% include divider.html %}

M27

Adaptive AI, intended for 20km and smaller maps. Est 700 1v1 ladder - 1200 global rating.


Author : maudlin27

M28

Competitive AI for FAF utilising advanced micro. Faster to run than M27 in teamgames, similar in difficulty. Estimated as challenging for a 700-1200 rated player. Supports FA, SC and Seraphim campaigns (as an AI teammate)


Author : maudlin27

RNGAI

AI aimed at the players wanting to learn 1v1 gameplay. It provides an avenue to play against something that emulates some of the methods ladder players use!.


Author : Relent0r

AI-Uveso

Adaptive AI with multiple sub AIs in order for players to have control on the behavior of the AI! The sub AIs are Adaptive, Overwhelm, Rush, Experimental and Easy!


Author : Uveso

AI-Swarm

Swarm is a bit of a middle grounded AI, it is regarded as the most solid economic AI. It tends to be heavy on land but it adapts to air and naval situations!


Author : AzraelianAngel

DilliDalli

Alpha Version : 1v1 specialist AI


Author : Softles
